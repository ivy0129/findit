Bloxdhop.io

Bloxdhop.io is an exciting 3D platformer game that challenges your reflexes and timing. Jump from block to block across challenging obstacle courses, avoiding gaps and hazards. With colorful graphics and intuitive controls, this game provides a perfect balance of casual fun and skill-based challenge.

4.8 (1,876 ratings)

How to Play

  1. Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  2. Press SPACE to jump
  3. Time your jumps carefully to land on the next platform
  4. Complete each level by reaching the finish line
  5. Collect coins to unlock new characters and levels

Pro Tips

  • Look ahead and plan your route before jumping
  • For longer jumps, gain momentum by running first
  • Moving platforms require precise timing - watch their pattern before jumping
  • Don't rush! Taking your time often leads to better results
  • Some levels have hidden shortcuts - explore the environment
