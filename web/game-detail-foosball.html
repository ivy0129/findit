Experience the excitement of table football in this realistic 3D game. Challenge friends, master your shots, and become a foosball champion with authentic physics and controls.

How to Play

  1. Use mouse to control your players
  2. Click to shoot the ball
  3. Switch between different rows
  4. Score goals to win
  5. Defend your goal

Pro Tips

  • Practice passing between rows
  • Learn to control ball speed
  • Use quick switches for defense
  • Master shooting angles
  • Watch opponent's moves
