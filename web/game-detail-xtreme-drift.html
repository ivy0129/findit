Xtreme Drift Racing

Master the art of drifting in this intense racing game. Control your car through challenging tracks, perform spectacular drifts, and compete against other racers in this thrilling driving experience.

4.6 (1,532 ratings)

How to Play

  1. Use arrow keys or WASD to control your car
  2. Press SPACE for handbrake (drifting)
  3. Time your drifts through corners
  4. Complete tracks within time limits
  5. Earn points for stylish drifts

Pro Tips

  • Start drifting before the corner
  • Use the handbrake sparingly
  • Master the throttle control
  • Chain drifts for bonus points
  • Practice each track multiple times
