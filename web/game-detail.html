Blockbuster Puzzle

Match and clear blocks in this addictive puzzle game. Challenge yourself with multiple levels and try to achieve the highest score possible.

4.5 (1,234 ratings)

How to Play

  1. Click or tap blocks to select them
  2. Match 3 or more blocks of the same color
  3. Clear blocks to earn points
  4. Try to achieve the highest score possible
  5. Complete levels to unlock new challenges

Pro Tips

  • Look for chain reactions to maximize points
  • Plan your moves ahead to create better combinations
  • Focus on clearing the bottom blocks first
  • Use special blocks when available
  • Keep an eye on the remaining moves
