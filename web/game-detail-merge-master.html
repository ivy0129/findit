Merge Master Tanks

Merge tanks to create more powerful units and dominate the battlefield in this strategic battle game. Combine different types of tanks, plan your attacks, and defeat enemy forces.

4.5 (1,243 ratings)

How to Play

  1. Drag and drop similar tanks to merge them
  2. Create stronger tanks by merging same-level units
  3. Deploy tanks strategically on the battlefield
  4. Defeat enemy waves to progress
  5. Earn coins to unlock new tanks

Pro Tips

  • Focus on merging tanks of the same type first
  • Keep higher level tanks at the back
  • Use coins wisely to upgrade your arsenal
  • Create a balanced mix of tank types
  • Plan your merges ahead of time
