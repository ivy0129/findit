Foosball 3D

Experience the excitement of table football in this realistic 3D game. Master your shots, compete against AI, and enjoy authentic foosball physics in this engaging sports game.

4.5 (1,876 ratings)

How to Play

  1. Use your mouse to control the rods and players
  2. Click and drag to rotate the rods
  3. Click to shoot the ball
  4. Score goals by getting the ball past your opponent's goalkeeper

Pro Tips

  • Master the timing of your shots for better accuracy
  • Use quick passes between players to create scoring opportunities
  • Learn to defend with your goalkeeper effectively
