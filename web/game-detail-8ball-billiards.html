8 Ball Billiards Classic

Master the art of pool in this realistic billiards game. Experience authentic physics, multiple game modes, and perfect your shots in this classic pool game.

5.0 (2,567 ratings)

How to Play

  1. Use your mouse to aim and click to shoot
  2. Control the power of your shot by holding the mouse button longer
  3. Pocket all your balls and the 8-ball to win
  4. Be careful not to pocket the 8-ball too early

Pro Tips

  • Plan your shots ahead and consider the position of other balls
  • Use English (spin) to control the cue ball's movement
  • Practice your break shot for better game starts
