Find It

Find It is an engaging hidden object puzzle game that challenges your observation skills and attention to detail. Search through intricately designed scenes to discover hidden objects within a time limit.

4.8 (2,156 ratings)

How to Play

  1. Look carefully at each scene to find the listed objects
  2. Click on objects when you find them
  3. Complete levels within the time limit
  4. Earn points for quick finds

Pro Tips

  • Scan the scene systematically from left to right
  • Pay attention to object shadows and reflections
  • Look for partially hidden objects
  • Use hints wisely when stuck
